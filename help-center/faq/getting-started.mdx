## Overview

Starting a new documentation project in ginjou is simple.

## Steps

1. Log in at `https://app.ginjou.app`.
2. Click "New Project" on your dashboard.
3. Enter your project name and description.
4. Click "Create".

Your project will appear in your project list. Click it to start adding docs.
