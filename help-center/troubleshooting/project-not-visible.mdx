---
title: Project Not Visible After Creation
description: What to do if your ginjou project does not appear.
---

## Symptoms

- Newly created project doesn't show in dashboard
- Can't access or see your project

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Refresh the dashboard">
    Reload the page or click the dashboard logo to refresh your project list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check filters">
    Ensure no search or filter hides your project.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log out and log in again">
    Sign out and back in to reload your workspace.
  </Step>
</Steps>
