## Overview

ginjou lets you organize docs into projects.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in">
    Go to `https://app.ginjou.app` and sign in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start a new project">
    Click the "New Project" button in your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill details">
    Enter a name and optional description for your project.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create and open">
    Click "Create". Your project is ready for docs!
  </Step>
</Steps>
