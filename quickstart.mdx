## Prerequisites

Before starting, ensure you have:

- A modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
- An email address for account verification
- Optional: A GitHub account for version control integration

<Callout kind="info">
  Ginjou works entirely in your browser. No downloads required.
</Callout>

## Create Your Ginjou Account

Sign up in under a minute.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Dashboard" icon="globe">
    Navigate to [https://app.ginjou.com](https://app.ginjou.com) and click **Sign Up**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details" icon="user">
    Provide your email, create a password, and verify your account via email link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Onboarding" icon="check-circle">
    Answer a few quick questions about your project to customize your workspace.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Use a strong password. Enable two-factor authentication in your [account settings](/introduction#settings).
</Callout>

## Set Up Your First Project

Create a new documentation space.

<Steps>
  <Step title="New Project" icon="plus">
    From the dashboard, click **New Project** and select **Documentation Space**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name It" icon="edit-3">
    Enter a name like `My Project Docs` and choose a slug (e.g., `my-project-docs`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Initialize" icon="sparkles">
    Ginjou auto-generates a starter structure with `introduction.mdx`, `quickstart.mdx`, and `faq.mdx`.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Add and Organize Initial Documents

Populate your space with content.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Markdown Page" icon="file-text">
    Click **New Page** > **Markdown**.

    ````markdown
    ```mdx
    ---
    title: My First Page
    description: Your custom content here.
    ---

    ## Welcome

    Start writing your docs!
    ```
    ````
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Embed Code" icon="code">
    Use the editor to add interactive examples.

    <CodeGroup tabs="JavaScript,Python">
    ````javascript
    console.log("Hello, Ginjou!");
    ````
    ````python
    print("Hello, Ginjou!")
    ````
    </CodeGroup>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

Drag pages in the sidebar to organize into folders. Use the search bar to find documents quickly.
